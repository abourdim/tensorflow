AI Mistakes Gallery

Even AI Gets It Wrong! 🤦

Real-world AI fails that teach us about data quality

❄️
The AI That Forgot Snow
An image classifier trained only on summer photos completely failed when shown winter scenes. It had never learned what snow looks like!
Why? → Training data was biased — only one season represented
Lesson: Data must represent ALL situations the AI will face.
🛑
Stop Sign Confusion
Researchers showed that adding small stickers to a stop sign could trick AI into seeing it as a speed limit sign.
Why? → Small changes humans ignore can fool AI completely
Lesson: AI sees patterns differently than humans — it can be tricked.
4️⃣
4s vs 9s
Handwriting AI often confuses digit 4 with 9 because they share similar features — an angled line and a vertical stroke.
Why? → Similar features between categories make classification harder
Lesson: Clear, distinct examples help AI learn better — your job matters!
🐕
Muffin or Chihuahua?
AI struggled to tell apart photos of chihuahuas and blueberry muffins — they look surprisingly similar from above!
Why? → Visual similarity across categories confuses pattern matching
Lesson: AI needs diverse examples to learn subtle differences.
