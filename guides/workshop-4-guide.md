# Workshop 4 Guide: "AI Decoded" (Parents & Teachers)

## Facilitator Section

### Learning Objectives
- Understand what AI/ML actually is
- Know what their kids are learning
- Feel confident facilitating workshops
- Understand AI ethics

### Session Timing (~45 min)
- **Interactive learning:** 25 min — 7 chapters with hands-on demos
- **Discussion:** 15 min — Ethics scenarios, Q&A
- **Certificate:** 5 min — Summary and next steps

### Tips
- Make adults comfortable with "not knowing"
- The sorting demo in Chapter 1 is a great ice-breaker
- Ethics scenarios work best as group discussions

## For Developers & Engineers

### Tools Used
- Lightweight interactive demos (pure JS)
- CSS animations for visual explanations
- No complex setup — just a browser

### Genius Project Examples (daily life AI)
- **Spam filters:** classifying emails for 20+ years
- **Google Translate:** 100+ languages via neural networks
- **Medical diagnosis:** assisting doctors with scan analysis
- **Traffic navigation:** AI predicting fastest routes
- **Accessibility:** AI generating image descriptions for the visually impaired
