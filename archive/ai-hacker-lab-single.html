🔌 Hardware Lab

micro:bit & ESP32-C3 projects with AI

🎮 Gesture Classifier [micro:bit | Beginner | 30min]

Train AI to recognize shake, tilt, spin gestures using the micro:bit's accelerometer via Web Bluetooth.

🌡️ Sensor Data Sorting [micro:bit | Beginner | 25min]

Collect real temperature & light data from different environments. Classify readings as hot/cold, bright/dark.

🌱 Smart Plant Monitor [ESP32-C3 | Intermediate | 45min]

Build an IoT device monitoring plant health. Soil moisture + light + temperature sensors feed AI classifier.

⚡ Edge AI Deployment [ESP32-C3 | Advanced | 60min]

Train in browser → export to TFLite → flash to ESP32-C3. AI runs on-chip, no internet needed.
